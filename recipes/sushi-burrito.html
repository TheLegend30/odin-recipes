Sushi Burrito

What is it?

Sushi Burrito: A tantalizing fusion of Japanese and Mexican cuisines, this dish wraps a generous serving of sushi ingredients—rice, fish, vegetables, and sauces—in a seaweed sheet, creating a handheld, flavorful delight with every bite.

Ingredients

  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Sushi rice
  • Sashimi-grade fish or cooked protein (e.g., salmon, tuna, shrimp, or tofu)
  • Vegetables (e.g., avocado, cucumber, carrot)
  • Soy sauce or tamari
  • Pickled ginger
  • Wasabi
  • Sesame seeds (optional)
  • Spicy mayo or other sushi sauces
  • Rice vinegar (for seasoning sushi rice)

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Slice fish and vegetables into thin strips.
  2. Lay Out Nori: Place a sheet of nori on a clean surface.
  3. Add Rice: Spread a layer of sushi rice evenly on the nori, leaving a border at the edges.
  4. Layer Ingredients: Arrange a variety of fish, vegetables, and any desired fillings along the center of the rice.
  5. Roll It Up: Carefully roll the nori and rice around the fillings, forming a burrito shape.
  6. Seal Edges: Moisten the edge of the nori with water to seal the burrito.
  7. Slice and Serve: Using a sharp knife, slice the sushi burrito into halves or thirds.
  8. Serve with Condiments: Present with soy sauce, pickled ginger, wasabi, and any additional sauces.
  9. Enjoy: Pick up your sushi burrito, dip, and savor the delicious fusion of flavors!
