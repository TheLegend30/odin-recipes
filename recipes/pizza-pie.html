Pizza Pizza Pie

What is it?

Delicious and savory, Pizza Pie is a mouthwatering culinary creation that combines a crisp, golden crust with a perfect blend of gooey cheese, zesty tomato sauce, and your favorite toppings.

A timeless comfort food that satisfies every palate.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives)
  • Olive oil (optional, for brushing the crust)
  • Italian herbs and spices (optional, for added flavor)

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Heat to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare Dough: Roll out pizza dough on a pan.
  3. Add Sauce: Spread tomato sauce, leaving a border.
  4. Top It: Sprinkle with mozzarella and favorite toppings.
  5. Bake: Cook until golden and bubbly, following dough instructions.
  6. Enjoy: Cool briefly, slice, and savor your homemade pizza pie!
