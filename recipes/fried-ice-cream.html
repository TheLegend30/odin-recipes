Fried Ice Cream

What is it?

Fried Ice Cream: A delightful dessert that defies expectations, featuring a scoop of ice cream encased in a crispy, golden shell. The contrast of warm and cold, crunchy and creamy makes for a uniquely satisfying treat.

Ingredients

  • Ice cream scoops
  • Cornflakes or breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon (optional)
  • Oil for frying
  • Toppings (e.g., chocolate syrup, whipped cream, cherry)

Steps

  1. Prepare Ice Cream: Scoop and freeze.
  2. Coat in Crumbs: Roll in crushed cornflakes with sugar.
  3. Double Coat (Optional): Repeat for extra crispiness.
  4. Freeze Again: Firm up in the freezer.
  5. Egg Wash: Dip in beaten egg.
  6. Quick Fry: Fry briefly until golden.
  7. Drain and Serve: Remove, drain, and enjoy the crispy delight.
  8. Optional Garnish: Add cinnamon and favorite toppings.
